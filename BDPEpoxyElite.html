Who we are

BDP Epoxy Elite is a South Florida coatings crew that treats prep like the job it actually is.

Most failed epoxy floors don't fail because of the resin — they fail because someone skipped the grind. We diamond-grind every slab so the coating bonds into the concrete instead of sitting on top of it, then build the system in layers and let it cure on schedule.

The result is a seamless, non-porous floor that shrugs off hot tires, spills, and traffic for years — and an install you can actually plan your week around. No surprises, no subcontractors, no mystery line items.

How it works

Five steps to a floor that lasts.

A floor is only as good as the prep underneath it. Here's exactly how we get from bare concrete to sealed and warrantied.

01

Consult & quote

We measure on site, assess the slab's condition and moisture, talk through finishes, and hand you a clear written estimate.

02

Diamond grinding

Mechanical surface prep opens the concrete's profile so the coating bonds into the slab — the single most important step.

03

Base coat & broadcast

We lay the color base and work flake or metallic by hand, building the system to the thickness your floor needs.

04

Topcoat & cure

A high-build clear seal locks everything in for a seamless, non-porous finish — then we let it cure on schedule.

05

Walk-through

We hand back a clean site and a sealed, warrantied floor, and walk you through care so it stays sharp for years.
